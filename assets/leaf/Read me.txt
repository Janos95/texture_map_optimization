Naming Convention

Prefixes:

T_ = Texture
SM_ = Static Mesh

Suffixes:

_A = Albedo
_AO = Ambient Occlusion 
_D = Displacement 
_N = Normal
_R = Roughness
_TR = Translucency
_R_AO_D = Roughness (Red Channel), Ambient Occlusion (Green Channel), Displacement (Blue Channel)
